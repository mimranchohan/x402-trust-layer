What is x402 Trust Layer

Raw payments are risky.
Trust is everything.

x402 Trust Layer is the control plane between AI agents and the open x402 marketplace. Every call decides whether to pay, whom to trust, which rail to use, and what to do when something goes wrong.

Built for agent fleets that handle real money — finance teams that need audit trails, developers wiring OpenDexter, and sellers who want verified attestations before accepting payment.
