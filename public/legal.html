1. What the service is

x402 Trust Layer ("the Service") provides advisory trust, security, and settlement-operations APIs that AI agents call before, during, and after x402 payments. Responses are signals and recommendations (e.g. a security grade, a trust score, an allow/deny suggestion). You decide whether to act on them.

2. No custody of funds

The Service is not a custodian. You pay your own counterparties with your own wallet over x402. "Escrow" features are server-side ledger/state machines for coordination and reporting; they do not take possession of your USDC or other assets.

3. No insurance or guarantee

"Liability attestation" and similar features produce signed cryptographic claims about declared merchant bonds — they are not underwritten insurance policies and guarantee no payout. The Service does not guarantee that a merchant is safe, that a payment will succeed, or that any signal is 100% accurate. Settlement also depends on third-party facilitators (e.g. Dexter, Coinbase CDP) whose availability is outside our control.

5. Payments
